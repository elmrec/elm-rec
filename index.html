Our Mission

To maintain and improve Elm Road Recreation Ground as a vibrant, safe, and enjoyable space for all members of our community to relax, play, and connect with nature.

Protect the park

Support litter picks, planting days, and practical improvements that keep Elm Road Recreation Ground safe, clean, and loved.

Grow local participation

Make it easy for neighbours to join in, hear about events early, and contribute without needing to join a committee first.

Create visible momentum

Turn volunteer effort into visible results, then document them so more residents can see what is happening in the park.

Why the park matters

Shared green space

Elm Road Recreation Ground is used for play, exercise, social time, and day-to-day breathing room in the neighbourhood.

What FERR focuses on

Practical improvement

The group concentrates on maintenance, planting, events, and small visible changes that improve how the park feels.

How people join in

Low-friction participation

You can attend a single event, volunteer occasionally, share ideas, or simply stay updated through the site and WhatsApp.
