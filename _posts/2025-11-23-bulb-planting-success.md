What a wonderful morning! Around 35 people from our local community came together to transform Elm Road Recreation Ground with a spectacular display of spring bulbs.

## A Community Effort

On Saturday, November 23rd, our community showed what can be achieved when we work together. Armed with trowels, enthusiasm, and a shared vision for a more beautiful park, volunteers of all ages planted approximately **1000 bulbs** across the recreation ground.

![Community volunteers planting bulbs](/assets/images/events/bulb-plant-2025-success/bulb-planting-02.jpg)
*Volunteers hard at work planting bulbs across the park*

## A Rainbow of Spring Colour

The variety of bulbs planted will create a stunning display come spring:
- Daffodils
- Crocus
- Tulips
- Allium
- Ixia
- Iris
- Muscari

Imagine the park bursting with colour in just a few months - it's going to be absolutely beautiful!

![Planting bulbs together](/assets/images/events/bulb-plant-2025-success/bulb-planting-03.jpg)
*Working together to create a beautiful spring display*
