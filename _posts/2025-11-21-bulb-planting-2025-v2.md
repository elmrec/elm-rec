Join us for a fun-filled morning of community gardening! Plant colorful spring bulbs, help clean up our park, and try your luck at finding the hidden golden bulb for a special prize. All ages and abilities welcome!

![Bulb Planting Event](/assets/images/events/bulb-plant-2025/bulb-plant.png)

## Event Details

### When & Where
- Date: Sunday, November 23, 2025
- Time: 11:00 AM
- Location: Elm Road Recreation Ground

### Activities
- **Spring Bulb Planting**: Help create a beautiful spring display by planting bulbs in designated areas around the park
- **Community Litter Pick**: Join our cleanup efforts to keep the park tidy and welcoming
- **Golden Bulb Hunt**: One lucky participant will find a special golden bulb - win an exciting prize!

### What to Bring
- Gardening gloves
- Small trowel or bulb planter (if you have one)
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- Water bottle

## How to Participate
1. Meet us at the main entrance at 11:00 AM
2. Sign in and collect equipment (we'll provide some trowels and bags)
3. Join a team for either bulb planting or litter picking
4. Help make our park beautiful!

## Additional Information
- **Free to Attend**: The event is completely free, though donations towards FERR's park improvement projects are greatly appreciated
- **Bring Your Own Bulbs**: Bring your own or donate for a change of finding the golden bulb!
- **Family Friendly**: Suitable for all ages and abilities. Children under 16 must be supervised by a responsible adult
- **Weather Policy**: The event will proceed in light rain but will be postponed in case of severe weather (check our social media for updates)

## Why Participate?
- **Make a Lasting Impact**: The bulbs you plant will bloom beautifully next spring, brightening the park for everyone to enjoy
- **Meet Your Neighbors**: Connect with fellow community members who care about our shared green space
- **Set a Positive Example**: Show children and others the importance of environmental stewardship
- **Enjoy the Outdoors**: Spend quality time in nature while doing something meaningful
