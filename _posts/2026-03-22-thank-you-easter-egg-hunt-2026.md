What an amazing day at Elm Road Recreation Ground.

On Sunday, March 22, 2026, more than **200 people** came along to enjoy our [Easter Egg Hunt](/events/easter-egg-hunt-2026/), and the sunshine was the perfect bonus. It was wonderful to see so many families, children, and neighbours coming together to celebrate spring.

![Families gathering for the Easter Egg Hunt at Elm Road Recreation Ground](/assets/images/events/easter-egg-hunt-2026-thank-you/easter-egg-hunt-2026-thank-you-02.jpg)
*Families enjoying the Easter Egg Hunt across the park*

## Thank You

A huge thank you to everyone who joined us and to all the volunteers who helped make the day run so smoothly. You made it truly special.

From the hunt itself to the activities around the park, it was a joy to see Elm Road Recreation Ground full of energy, laughter, and community spirit.

## Supporting the Park

If you enjoyed the event and would like to support Elm Road Recreation Ground, you can [make a donation here](/donate/). Any donations go directly towards improving the park for our whole community.

If you would like to get involved in future events or volunteering, please visit our [contact page](/contact/) or join our [WhatsApp group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/FZ9KMjIYmGNIgRolTgUVxb).
