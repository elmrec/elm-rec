## What you will find here
- Updates on current work happening in the park
- Event listings for family activities and volunteer sessions
- Background on the group and the people running it
- Clear ways to contact FERR or join the community channels

## What we are trying to achieve
- Keep the recreation ground welcoming, useful, and visibly cared for
- Build stronger local participation around the park
- Turn community effort into lasting improvements
- Make it easier for residents to know what is happening and how to help

## How to get started
- Browse the [Events](/events/) page if you want the fastest route into participation
- Visit [About](/about/) if you want to understand the group properly
- Use [Contact](/contact/) if you want to volunteer, ask a question, or share an idea
- Join the WhatsApp group for reminders and live updates
